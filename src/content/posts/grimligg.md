## Grimligg Ironmask
Grimligg Ironmask is a steadfast warrior from the Heron Clan, reknown in the northern marshlands. He has sworn an oath of the Heron, covering his identity with armor, committing himself fully to carrying out quests and legendary feats on behalf of his people. A force of nature, he is resolute, fierce, and a faithful companion to those who stand by him. 

## Currently
Upon hearing news of Jormund Osrigard’s fate, Grimligg journeys north toward The Wolf Lands, venturing alone as he leaves the marsh behind. As he sets forth on his path, his companions appear one by one, without uttering a word, they bow deeply and present him with their finest weapons for the journey.
