I am in love with [Crown & Skull](https://www.runehammer.online/crown-skull-rpg) by Runehammer. I know that this is going to be the main rule system for my ttrpg group as well as a few solo play plans for myself. I'm a miniature painter first before everything else in the hobby, so I am using this as my inspiration for what is likely the next 6 months to a year of my painting goals. I want to have every monster, npc, and pc of course painted and ready for when the d4 calls. I am tracking my progress in the table below. Found means I have found the mini or STL I a going to use, then built and painted is obvious.
